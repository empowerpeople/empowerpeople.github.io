Garrett Adelstein Versus Robbi Jade Lew

Video: https://youtu.be/9NNKjWscKWo

Garrett is certain that Robbi Jade Lew cheated in this hand. He made her give the money she just won back to him. Was this the right thing to do? There are lots of theories about her cheating. But many people don't consider that these theories have to be compared to the theories of her innocence. If you have a great theory that she cheated, there may be an even better one that she is innocent. Often the exact same evidence that strongly supports a theory of her guilt, supports even more strongly a theory of her innocence.
